Bread

Bread is a staple food in many cultures around the world. It is made from a simple mixture of flour, water, yeast, and salt. Different varieties of bread can be made by using different types of flour, different baking methods, and adding additional ingredients such as sugar, herbs, and spices.

Rice

Rice is a staple food for over half of the world's population. It is a versatile grain that can be cooked in many different ways and is a staple ingredient in cuisines worldwide. There are many different types of rice, including white, brown, and black rice, each with its own unique flavor, texture, and nutritional properties.

Nutella

Nutella is a popular chocolate and hazelnut spread that was first created in Italy in the 1940s. It has since become a beloved ingredient for sweet and savory dishes all over the world. Nutella is known for its smooth and creamy texture, rich chocolate and hazelnut flavor, and versatility in cooking and baking.
